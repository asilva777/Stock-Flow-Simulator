Stock–Flow Simulator

Think of it as a flight simulator for LGU finance: test policy choices & see what might happen.

How to Use

  1. Step 1: Explore on the sliders and buttons:
    Use the sliders & buttons below to test decisions.
  2. Step 2: Understand the Elements
    Stock: accumulated reserves
    Inflows: money in (taxes, IRA)
    Outflows: money out (expenses, debt)
  3. Step 3: Adjust Rates
    Move sliders, apply shocks, or run scenarios.
  4. Step 4: Observe
    Watch how the chart changes over time.
  5. Step 5: Try Scenarios
    Disaster shock, business boom, or tax reform.
  6. Step 6: Reflect
    Discuss: what had biggest impact? what surprised us?
